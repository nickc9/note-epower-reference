## Overview

Sizes are identical on every grade including NISMO/NISMO S (verified from
Nissan's own FAQ):

| Position | Length | Refill width |
|---|---|---|
| Driver (right) | **650 mm** | 10 mm |
| Passenger | **300 mm** | 10 mm |
| Rear | **305 mm** | 6 mm |

You can replace either the complete blade (easier; e.g. PIAA WMR650/WMR300)
or just the rubber refill in the existing frame (cheaper; e.g. NWB
MB65GN/MB30GN front, GR41-TN30G rear). NWB is the JDM OE supplier, so NWB
refills restore factory behaviour.

## Full blade swap (front)

1. Lift the wiper arm off the glass until it locks upright.
2. Rotate the blade perpendicular to the arm, press the release tab on the
   U-hook clip, and slide the blade down out of the hook.
3. Slide the new blade up into the hook until it clicks. Lower the arm
   gently — never let it snap onto bare glass.

## Refill-only swap

1. With the blade off (or on the arm, upright), find the closed end of the
   frame and slide the old rubber out from the open end, releasing the
   little metal spines' grip.
2. Transfer the two metal spines to the new rubber if it doesn't include
   them (NWB refills usually do).
3. Slide the new rubber in until the end notch clicks into the frame claw
   — give it a firm tug to confirm it's locked. An unlocked refill will
   fly out at speed on the first wet motorway drive.

## Rear

Same U-hook principle, smaller blade. The 6 mm rear refill is narrower
than the fronts — don't try to force a 10 mm front refill into it.
