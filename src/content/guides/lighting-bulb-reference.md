## Headlights by grade — the short version

**e-POWER NISMO and NISMO S** (verified, Nissan's own equipment sheet +
FAQ): **LED low beam** standard — projector type with auto-leveling and
integrated LED position lamp — plus high-beam assist, intelligent
auto-light, and the NISMO LED "hyper daylight" DRL. **The high beam is
still a halogen HB3 (9005) 60 W** even on these cars.

**e-POWER X / S**: halogen **H11 55 W** low beam, HB3 high beam.
**Medalist**: reported LED + fog standard (single-source, probable).

The petrol NISMO/NISMO S use halogen low beams with a manual leveler —
another quick way to tell the two NISMO S's apart.

## Full bulb table (verified: Nissan FAQ + independent fitment table)

| Position | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Low beam | LED (NISMO/NISMO S) or H11 55 W | LED = assembly, not a bulb swap |
| High beam | HB3 (9005) 60 W halogen | All grades |
| Fog lamps | H8 35 W (H11 55 W option on some trims) | Fitment varies by grade/option |
| Position/clearance | LED (LED-headlamp cars) or T10 | |
| Front turn signal | PY21W (S25 amber) | Filament, owner-serviceable |
| Tail | W5W (T10) | Filament |
| Brake / high-mount | LED (LED-headlamp cars) | Not a simple bulb swap |
| Rear turn signal | WY21W (T20 amber) | Filament |
| Reverse | W16W (T16) | Filament |
| License plate | W5W (T10) | Filament |
| Interior/room (front & middle) | T10 | Rear room lamp type unconfirmed |

No rear fog lamp has been confirmed on any JDM HE12 grade (normal for
JDM-market cars).

## If an LED headlamp dies

It's an assembly replacement, not a bulb. A genuine LH headlamp assembly
for the Note e-POWER family (26060-5WK5A, current variant 26060-5WK5D)
runs about **AU$1,000+ per side** in the Amayama genuine catalogue —
though whether that exact number is the LED or halogen spec is
unconfirmed, so order via a dealer with your frame number. Budget
accordingly, and check headlamp condition when buying an import.

## LED conversions (halogen grades only)

If your car is an X/S with H11 halogens, JP owners widely fit H11 LED
kits (Polarg kits ~¥8,000 via Amazon JP; fcl. is another popular brand).
One documented fitment quirk on later E12-family halogen housings: the
low-beam socket is a tubular design with a waterproof cap that must be
properly re-seated, and the insertion hole sits slightly offset — check
the fit before buying. **NISMO/NISMO S owners: your low beam is already
LED — this section isn't for your car.**

Fog lamps are a dealer retrofit option even on lower trims (an owner log
documents a ~3-hour genuine fog retrofit on an X grade). Genuine fog
part numbers for the HE12 remain unconfirmed.
